PolyHack 2022

About

PolyHack is The Hong Kong Polytechnic University’s largest hackathon. It is an Asia-wise competition organized by students. At PolyHack, we want to help you grow and bring your imaginations into reality. Regardless of your background, you will be able to participate with resources and mentors we will provide to help you progress with your projects. We will have free workshops, mentorships, prizes, games and more to help you learn new techniques for your future projects or just for you to explore outside of a classroom. Don’t have a team, or even an idea? We’ll help you expand your creativity to build an exceptional project!

Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is a Hackathon?

Hackathon is a derivative of Hack + Marathon where a large number of people gather to sprint through the production of a software project within a given time. There are workshops and mentors to guide you along your journey.

Who is organizing PolyHack?

PolyHack is organized by a group of students from the Google Developer Student Club PolyU.
Check out _____ to learn more about the organizers.

What is different about PolyHack?

PolyHack was made to help the students grow their knowledge beyond a normal classroom setting. Our hackathon is longer than the normal three day hack where we will offer extensive mentorship in order to shape your ideas into a market competitive solution. We will support you to become an entrepreneur with your product through mentorship, training and funding.

Application

Who can apply?

If you’re in your junior or senior year of highschool, enrolled in a college or university, you are eligible!

Can we apply as a team?

You may enter the hackathon as a team; however, each of your teammates will have to apply individually.

What does it cost?

PolyHack is free for all applicants. We do not expect any money from you, all we ask for is passion and enthusiasm to learn and expand your thinking! Rather, we will be giving you free workshops, mentorships and even prizes at the end!

Event

What if I don’t have a team or idea?

There is nothing to worry about. We will help you find teammates to join you on the journey and also mentors who will help you think of a project idea before and during the event.

Do I have to know how to code to attend?

You may enter the hackathon as a team; however, each of your teammates will have to apply individually.

What does it cost?

Not at all! We have alternative ways in which you can make projects with the platform we provide. We will have workshops to help you learn the platform so that your projects stand competitive!

Logistics

What if I’m an International Student not in Hong Kong?

Due to the current pandemic, all events will be held in a virtual mode so just make sure you have a strong internet connection and you are good to go!
